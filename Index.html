WELCOME

In 2004, the PITC-Pharma Inc. (PPI), in coordination with the DOH and the FDA, launched the Botika ng Bayan (BNB) program which entails legally qualified privately-operated retail outlets nationwide to provide consumers low-cost and high-quality medicines.

To promote equity in health by ensuring the availability and accessibility of affordable, safe and effective, quality essential drugs to all, with priority for marginalized, underserved, critical and hard to reach areas.
